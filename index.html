PROCESS

 
Research
Sketching & wireframeing
Prototyping
Usability Testing

I most commonly design using the double diamond process. During the discovery phase, I thoroughly research the problem using techniques such as competitive analysis, user interviews and surveys. In the define phase, I use techniques such as affinity mapping, user journeys and personas to set a clear design goal. Throughout the develop phase skills like sketching, wireframing, prototyping and usability testing to build, test and validate my designs.
